About the platform

Richfield Connect empowers academic belonging.

Richfield Connect is a front-end prototype built for Richfield Graduate Institute of Technology students. It simulates a campus-focused social network where learners can register a profile, present their study identity, and share short updates with their peers.

The experience is intentionally lightweight and stores all information in browser localStorage. This makes the application simple to demo, easy to navigate, and fully functional without any backend services.

Core goals

  • Promote digital identity for students.
  • Encourage academic community interaction.
  • Demonstrate modern front-end techniques with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery.
  • Provide a responsive design suitable for mobile, tablet, and desktop users.

Richfield Connect reflects professional, student-centred Richfield branding.
